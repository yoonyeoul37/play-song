Privacy Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2026  |  Last Updated: June 1, 2026

KNEXM Co., Ltd. (hereinafter "Company") values the privacy of CatSong app (hereinafter "App") users and complies with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

1. Information We Collect

The App uses only the minimum necessary information as follows:

  • Device storage access permission (to read music files)
  • Media file access permission (album art, video files)
  • In-app settings (theme color, text size, favorites, etc. – stored on device only)

The Company does not collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.).

2. Purpose of Collection and Use

  • Storage permission is used to play music/video files stored on your device within the App.
  • Collected information is not used for any purpose other than providing app functionality.

3. Retention Period

App settings (favorites, playlists, etc.) are stored only on your device and are not transmitted to the Company's servers. All data will be deleted from your device when you uninstall the App.

4. Third-Party Sharing

The Company does not share your personal information with third parties. Exceptions may occur only when required by law.

5. Internet Access and Lyrics Feature

The App may send song titles and artist names to an external API (lrclib.net) for the lyrics search feature. This information is used solely for lyrics retrieval and is not stored.

6. Your Rights

  • You can delete stored data (favorites, playlists, etc.) at any time through the App's settings.
  • Uninstalling the App will delete all app data stored on your device.
